Privacy Policy

This policy explains how {{ site.title }} ("we", "us") collects, uses and protects the personal information you provide to us. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Information we collect

When you contact us through our website, by telephone or by email, we may collect your name, email address, telephone number and any details you choose to share about your project. We do not collect special-category data.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare quotations, deliver our services and keep in touch about your project. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except trusted suppliers and trades where necessary to carry out the work you have requested.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file stored on your device. We use two kinds:

Essential cookies are needed for the website to work and to remember your cookie choice. These are always on and do not require your consent.

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are most popular) so we can improve it. These are only set after you have given consent via our cookie banner. We do not use cookies for advertising, and we do not sell or share this data.

When you first visit, our banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can change your decision at any time — reopen your cookie preferences — or block and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We keep enquiry and project information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and to meet our legal and accounting obligations, after which it is securely deleted.
